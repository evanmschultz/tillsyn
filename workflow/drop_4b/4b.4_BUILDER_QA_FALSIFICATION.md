3. **Test coverage — empty-string package element.** `Packages = ["", "pkg2"]` not exercised. Domain-layer guarantee per spec; no gate-level pin. PQA-4 surfaces this; non-blocking for 4b.4.

None of the NITs invalidate the implementation; all are test-rigor enhancements that could be picked up in 4b refinements or 4b.7 wiring.
