- 3.1 **Stale gopls diagnostic, not a defect.** The builder report flagged `recordTemplateRejectionAudit` and `templateNestingDecision` as "unused" per gopls. Both functions ARE called from `service.go:631` and `service.go:641` respectively — verified by LSP `findReferences` and confirmed by `mage ci` linking + executing the audit-trail tests. Required since droplet 3.2 made the field required at `NewActionItem`. Not a 3.16-scope finding; just confirms test discipline.
