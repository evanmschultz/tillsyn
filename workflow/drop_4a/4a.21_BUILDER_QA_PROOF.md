**Action for 4a.23 builder:** when wiring `*app.Service` into `dispatcher.NewDispatcher`, either (a) add a thin public `Service.GetActionItem(ctx, id)` wrapper around `s.repo.GetActionItem`, or (b) inject a small adapter struct with the four methods the monitor needs (Get/List/Move/Update).
