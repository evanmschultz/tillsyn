## Bonus Coverage (Beyond Spec)

The builder shipped seven additional tests beyond the four required by AC7. Documented as design choices in the worklog (§Input Validation, §Closed-Enum Errors). Each adds explicit-failure pinning rather than relaxing the contract:

- `TestBuildSpawnCommandReturnsErrNoAgentBindingForEmptyCatalog` — nil-map path on zero-value catalog.
