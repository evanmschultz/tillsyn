- 1.2 **A2 â€” `bufio.Scanner` 1 MiB buffer overflow: NIT (gap, not counterexample).** `scanner.Buffer(make([]byte, 0, 64*1024), monitorScannerMaxBytes)` caps token size at 1 MiB.
